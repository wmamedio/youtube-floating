## Data Collection

YouTube Floating Player does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. The extension runs entirely in your browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Permissions

The extension requests access to `www.youtube.com/watch*` pages solely to inject a content script that pins the video player to the top of the viewport when you scroll. No other websites are accessed.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The extension contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising code.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/wmamedio/youtube-floating/issues.
